Features

It works across any browser capable device.

CURRENTLY SUPPORTING:
IE6, IE7, IE8, Safari, Chrome,
Opera & Firefox

Access to the service while both
online and offline.

Data can be retreived offline and syncs when an change is detected online.

Updating your menus made easy.

Our Content Management System seamlessly integrates with your content using our edit-in-place user interface.

Easy to emplement high- definition platform.

Our platform is built to run off of Computers and Mini Tv’s for a seamless plug and play experience.

Built using the latest and greatest web technologies.

The Hatchware Platform is built on top of CounchDB, Therapist.js, HTML5, CSS3, & Javascript

Easily distribute your menus to multiple locations.

Management of your menus is easily distributed across our fault tolerant cloud.
